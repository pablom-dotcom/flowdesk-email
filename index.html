FAQ

Common questions

What kinds of sequences can this generate?
Welcome sequences, client onboarding, post-proposal follow-ups, cold lead nurture, re-engagement, product launches, and more. Just describe your goal in question 1 and the AI figures out the best structure.
How many emails does it generate?
Between 3 and 7 emails, depending on the sequence type. Welcome and onboarding sequences typically get 3–5 emails; nurture and sales sequences get 5–7, with logical send timing for each.
Do I need to know copywriting?
Not at all. Just answer the five questions in plain language — describe what you do, who you're emailing, and what you want them to do. The AI handles the writing.
Can I edit the emails before sending?
Yes — the PDF download gives you the full text of every email, which you can paste into your email platform (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, etc.) and edit as needed before sending.
